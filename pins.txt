Rpi Pins (physical, not GPIO)
=============================
1     GPS PWR
2     Accelerometer PWR
3     Accelerometer SDA
5     Accelerometer SCL
6     Accelerometer GND
8     GPS RX
10    GPS TX
14    GPS GND
15    Start/stop switch (GPIO 22)
17    Start/stop switch PWR
18    Start/stop light (GPIO 24)
20    Start/stop light GND
39    Mic cable ground
